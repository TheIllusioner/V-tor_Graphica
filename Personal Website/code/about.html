How to operate the train

Use this train to navigate to different webpages! Follow these steps:

  1. Click on the arrows above the train doors to choose your destination
  2. Click on the name of your destination on the sign between the arrows
  3. When the train stops and the doors open, click on the outside space to exit the train!

On the posters around this seat you can find a sitemap if you get lost!
